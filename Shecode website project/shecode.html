My story

I've always liked trying to figure out hard problems, and math and logic come naturally to me. It's a lot of work, but I enjoy the challenge. So when I was first introduced to Java in high school's computer class, it piqued my interest. It's like putting together a puzzle, except more so. Therefore, I made it a point to excel in any and all tasks having to do with that subject. Then I went to college and majored in engineering because math is my favorite subject (or so I tell myself), but I ended up with a degree in electronics and telecommunications instead of computer science, which was what I really wanted to do. In the past, I've dabbled in freelancing as a social media manager, a graphic designer, and a customer service rep, but ultimately I always found my way back to the realm of programming. A Facebook page advertising Python programming sprung up while I was there, so I attended their seminar, and it reawakened my interest in computer science that I had as a young girl. I continued to join their boot camp, StarTrek. Thus, here I am, your Software Engineer, ready to code for you.
